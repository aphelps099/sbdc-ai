SBDC AI Survey Results

Understanding AI adoption, challenges, and opportunities across SBDC centers. This survey captures insights from SBDC professionals about their current use of AI tools, barriers to adoption, and future plans for integrating AI into their client services.

About This Survey

The SBDC AI Survey was conducted to assess the current state of artificial intelligence adoption within Small Business Development Centers. The survey explores how SBDC advisors are using AI tools in their work, what challenges they face, and what resources they need to effectively leverage AI for client services.

The results provide valuable insights into training needs, common use cases, and barriers to adoption that can help shape future AI initiatives across the SBDC network.
