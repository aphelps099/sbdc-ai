About SBDC

The NorCal Small Business Development Center (SBDC) is part of a national network that provides no-cost business consulting and low-cost training to help entrepreneurs start, grow, and succeed. We are one of the pillars of America's small business support ecosystem, funded in part through a cooperative agreement with the U.S. Small Business Administration.

NorCal SBDC AI Policy Quick Reference Guide

Effective: 11.22.2025

Green Light Approved
Secure AI Tools
  • ChatGPT Teams SBDC account (data protected)
  • Notebook LM (approved for research synthesis)
Appropriate Applications
  • Drafting communications, reports & session notes
  • Creating training materials
  • Process optimization
  • Aggregate data analysis (de-identified only)
Red Light Prohibited
Absolute Prohibitions
  • NEVER upload client data/PII to non-SBDC tools
  • NEVER let AI replace human judgment in advising
Data Privacy
  • No client names, SSNs, financials in unapproved tools
  • No screenshots containing sensitive data
Content Integrity
  • No unverified AI-generated facts in client materials
  • No AI-generated legal or financial advice
Violations impact: Federal grant compliance (2 CFR 130), client trust, and disciplinary action. When in doubt, ask before using AI with any client information.
